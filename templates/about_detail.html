More About JKUSOS

JKUSOS is more than just a club — it's a launchpad for personal and academic growth. We believe in giving every student the opportunity to lead, learn, and make meaningful contributions to society through data and innovation.

Registration Fee

The current registration fee for new members is KES 300. This fee helps support club activities, materials, and administrative costs.

What You Get as a Member

  • Access to exclusive data science and statistics workshops
  • Certificate-backed projects and research opportunities
  • Networking sessions with alumni and industry professionals
  • Participation in field trips, conferences, and outreach

Join Us

Membership is open to all students, regardless of course. Whether you're pursuing statistics, data science, engineering, or just curious about data — you're welcome to join us.
